About Me

Andrew Reedy is a 26 year old born and raised in Tallmadge, Ohio. While growing up he was very interested in the outdoors, music, playing sports and anything with an engine.

After graduating from UIC, Matt moved out to Cleveland where he started his career as a Sales Support Specialist at Park Place Tech. After only a year, he worked his way up to Contracts Admin Management - overseeing a team of 8 individuals, responsible for training, and being the liaison between our Legal and Financial departments for our customers and sales staff.

Currently Andrew is chasing his passion for technology and is currently enrolled in CSU's coding bootcamp. When he's not atteneding class he still finds time for his old passions and friends in Cleveland Ohio.
